A little bit about our owner | Kristina Liburd

My home base is Southern Massachusetts with my small growing family. My journey into tech started with a career in law specializing in intellectual property. As I explored the different facets of copyright, trademark and patents, I realized that my facisnation with technology was deep-rooted. I wanted to learn how to become one of the developers I admired so well.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development with my company SweetSpot.Tech, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect match.
